<h3 align="left">About Me</h3>

I am a Computer Science undergraduate at **NIT Durgapur**, deeply interested in building systems that operate at the intersection of **software, hardware, and intelligence**. My approach to engineering is grounded in **first principles** understanding how systems work from the lowest level before abstracting upward. I enjoy reasoning about constraints, trade-offs, and real-world behavior rather than treating technology as a black box.My interests span robotics, embedded systems, computer vision, and system-level software, with a focus on creating reliable, efficient, and interpretable solutions. I am particularly drawn to problems where software must interact with the physical world and operate under uncertainty.
